# Pyrogue Walkthrough

This walk-through keeps the domain vocabulary close to the data instead of burying it in prose.

| Case | Focus | Score | Lane |
| --- | --- | ---: | --- |
| baseline | turn pressure | 120 | watch |
| stress | map entropy | 150 | ship |
| edge | collision risk | 94 | hold |
| recovery | visibility | 225 | ship |
| stale | turn pressure | 263 | ship |

Start with `stale` and `edge`. They create the widest contrast in this repository's fixture set, which makes them better review anchors than the middle cases.

The useful comparison is `turn pressure` against `collision risk`, not the raw score alone.
